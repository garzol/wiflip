Switch matrix dock window

Usage

This is the emulation window for switches

Every black ball represents a switch of the switch matrix. Clicking any of these ball is equivalent to hitting the corresponding switch of the pin for 300ms or so.

There is a way to maintain a switch closed. Simply triple-click the ball and it will stay closed (appearing in red color).

⚠ Be careful with this feature, because in some cases, it can lead the pinball to activate a coil permanently. This might burn it, or the controlling transistor. For example, 3-clicking on Ball home switch will trigger the ball home coil rejector permanently until the switch is seen open.

Calques

Select the calque you wish to get the corresponding role of switches, in relationship with the corresponding game.

Hovering the mouse on balls triggers the corresponding tooltip.
